Where to find these numbers?

Reference Number

You can find your 14-digit Reference Number in the top-right corner of any old physical MEPCO bill just below your name and address.

Customer ID

The 10-digit Customer ID is also printed on the top section of your bill, normally directly above the reference number box.

Need Help? If you cannot find either number, please contact your nearest MEPCO sub-division office.
